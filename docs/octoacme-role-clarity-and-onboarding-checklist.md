## Purpose
Ensure new team members understand their role, responsibilities, and how they interact with other roles on the project. This checklist promotes clarity, reduces onboarding time, and establishes accountability from day one.

## When to use
- When a new team member joins the project
- When someone transitions to a new role
- During project kickoff to establish role clarity across the team
- As a reference during role confusion or accountability gaps

---

## Role Definition & Clarity

### For the New Team Member
- [ ] Review the relevant persona definition in `docs/octoacme-roles-and-personas.md`
- [ ] Understand your core responsibilities and goals
- [ ] Identify your typical communication patterns and channels
- [ ] Review the Role Interaction Matrix to see how you collaborate with other roles

### For the Team Lead or Manager
- [ ] Assign the team member to the appropriate persona/role
- [ ] Clarify any role-specific expectations or variations for this project
- [ ] Introduce the team member to their key interaction points (see matrix)
- [ ] Schedule 1:1 introductions with critical collaborators

---

## Project Context & Access

- [ ] Access granted to project repository and documentation
- [ ] Added to relevant communication channels (Slack, Teams, email lists)
- [ ] Access to project board (GitHub Projects, Jira, etc.)
- [ ] Permissions configured for CI/CD and deployment tools (if applicable)
- [ ] Calendar invites sent for recurring meetings (standups, planning, reviews)

## Role-Specific Onboarding

### For Developers
- [ ] Review coding standards and PR conventions
- [ ] Set up local development environment
- [ ] Run existing tests and understand CI pipeline
- [ ] Identify Technical Lead for design review questions
- [ ] Pair with another developer on first task

### For Product Managers
- [ ] Review product roadmap and current backlog
- [ ] Understand success metrics and how they're tracked
- [ ] Meet with key stakeholders and business analysts
- [ ] Align with engineering leads on upcoming priorities

### For Project Managers
- [ ] Review project plan, timeline, and milestones
- [ ] Access risk register and dependency tracking tools
- [ ] Understand stakeholder reporting cadence and format
- [ ] Meet with Scrum Master to align on team rhythm

### For Stakeholders
- [ ] Understand decision gates and approval processes
- [ ] Clarify escalation paths and communication preferences
- [ ] Review project one-pager and success metrics
- [ ] Schedule regular checkpoint meetings with PM

### For Business Analysts
- [ ] Access requirements documentation and templates
- [ ] Meet with stakeholders to understand business context
- [ ] Align with Product Manager on prioritization approach
- [ ] Connect with QA Specialist on acceptance criteria standards

### For Scrum Masters
- [ ] Understand team's current sprint cadence and ceremonies
- [ ] Review team velocity and historical sprint data
- [ ] Identify current impediments and action items
- [ ] Meet with previous Scrum Master for handoff (if applicable)

### For QA Specialists
- [ ] Review test strategy and existing test coverage
- [ ] Access test environments and test data
- [ ] Understand defect tracking and reporting process
- [ ] Align with developers on testing workflow and automation

### For Technical Leads
- [ ] Review architecture documentation and decision records (ADRs)
- [ ] Understand technical debt backlog and priorities
- [ ] Meet with development team to assess skill levels
- [ ] Connect with Product Manager on technical roadmap

## Accountability & RACI

- [ ] Understand your role in the project RACI matrix
- [ ] Identify decisions you are Responsible for
- [ ] Identify decisions you are Accountable for
- [ ] Know who to Consult and who to Inform for your key activities
- [ ] Clarify handoff points with other roles

---

## First Week Goals

- [ ] Attend all recurring team meetings
- [ ] Complete at least one meaningful contribution (code, doc, analysis, etc.)
- [ ] Ask questions and document answers for future team members
- [ ] Provide feedback on onboarding process to improve this checklist
- [ ] Schedule 30-day check-in with manager or team lead

---

## Resources & Support

- [ ] Know who to ask for help (buddy, mentor, manager)
- [ ] Understand escalation process for blockers
- [ ] Bookmark key documentation and tools
- [ ] Join team social channels and introduce yourself

---

## Continuous Improvement

After completing onboarding:
- What went well?
- What was confusing or unclear?
- What resources or information were missing?
- How can we improve this checklist for the next person?

Document your feedback and share it with the Project Manager or Scrum Master.
